What is Tandoori?
Tandoori food preparation takes its name from the Tandoor-an oven made with Indian clay. At about 30 feet deep and a diameter of 40″ narrowing to about 18″ at the top it is used for baking bread and roasting lamb chciken or shrimp. This process seals in the flavor of the meat preserving the unmistakable spicy taste which comes from a 24-hour marinating process.
